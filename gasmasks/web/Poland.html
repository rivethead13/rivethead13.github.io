  • Type:MC1
  • Translation:
  • Aliases: n/a
  • Date: c. 1960s/1970s
  • Frequency: Uncommon
  • Country: Poland
  • Use: Civilian/Military
  • Refrence: PO-009
  • Price: $35
  • Notes: The Polish MC-1 was a simply designed civilian mask very similar to the Czech. CM-3. Some versions of this mask may have also been used by the military. A 40mm NATO thread filter canister was attached directly to the face piece with an exhale valve positioned directly above it. Most early versions used a rubber head harness while some later versions used a fabric/elastic blended harness for better comfort and fit. The MC1 is fairly common on the surplus market and can usually be found in complete un-issued condition.
