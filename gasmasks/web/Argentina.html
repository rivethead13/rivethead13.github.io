  • Notes: This mask, made by Pirelli Rubber of Italy, was either manufactured by a facility in Argentina or exported and used in Argentina during WWII. It features a long breathing tube and a large heavy filter canister. Circular exhale valves are also present on the sides of the mask. The face piece design is similar to the AGM style masks used in Italy and Belgium during WWII. This mask is now quite difficult to find on the collectors market.
