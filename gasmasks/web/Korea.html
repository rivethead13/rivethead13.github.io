  • Notes: The K1 is a newer military NBC respirator made by Samgong of Korea. It is most likely an upgrade for the earlier KM9A1, which was a copy of the US M9A1. It features a comfortable elastic head harness and the ability to attach a canteen to the right side of the face piece. A protective rubber hood is also issued with the K1. Several of these masks have been exported and sold new in various countries.
