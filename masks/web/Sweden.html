  • Type:Folkgasmask Typ 33
  • Translation:Peoples Gas Mask Type 33
  • Aliases: n/a
  • Date: 1940
  • Frequency: Rare
  • Country: Sweden
  • Use: Military/Civilian/Industrial
  • Refrence: SW-010
  • Price: $90
  • Notes: The Swedish Typ 33 is a modern civilian mask that designed to offer a higher lever of protection and comfort. The mask was made from soft gray rubber, and it featured a single plastic visor and an elastic head harness. The mask came with a single fixed filter which was integrated into the front of the mask. An inner nose cup was also installed to reduce fogging. Unlike the earlier Swedish civilian masks, the Typ 33 included an exhale valve on the bottom of the mask, which certainly improved the level of protection. The Typ 33 was issued in an oval shaped, gray plastic tub.
  • Type:Skyddsmask 51
  • Translation: Protective Mask 51
  • Aliases: n/a
  • Date: 1960s
  • Frequency: Common/Uncommon
  • Country: Sweden
  • Use: Military
  • Refrence: SW-011
  • Price: $25
  • Notes: The Skyddsmask 51 is a licensed copy of the US M9 Gas Mask, and overall it is quite similar. The Skyddsmask 51 was made from dark green rubber, and had a larger and longer exhale valve guard. The mask came in two versions, an early version that could only use an old style 60mm thread filter canister, and a newer version that had a double threaded inlet, which allowed both 60mm and 40mm thread canisters to be attached. The Skyddsmask 51 is quite common on the surplus market in the US, and can often be found in mint, un-issued condition.
