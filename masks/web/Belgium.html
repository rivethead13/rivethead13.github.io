  • Notes: The Belgian L.702 is quite possibly the most commonly found Belgian gas mask from WWII. There were several versions of this mask made between the 1930s and 1950s, with the type pictured at right being the most common. One version of this mask designated the L.702 "Typ Activ" was used by Civil Defense and Air Raid teams.
