  • Type:TF1 (early version)
  • Translation:
  • Aliases: MF1
  • Date: c. 1960s
  • Frequency: Rare/Scarce
  • Country: China
  • Use: Military
  • Refrence: CH-004
  • Price: $45
  • Notes: The Chinese TF1 is a very close copy of the common Soviet ShM 'helmet' type gas mask. It looks almost identical, however the TF1 uses a plastic inlet/outlet valve assembly instead of a metal one. Early TF1 type masks were made of a brownish rubber where later versions were made from a gray colored rubber (see below). The example pictured to the right is missing the breathing tube and also the filter canister, which was most likely very similar to the M64 (above). This early version of the TF1 is more difficult to find today than the updated version, which was imported in quantity after the Sept. 11th terrorist attacks and the anthrax mailings that followed.

The M69 was rarely exported, making the mask quite difficult to find outside of China.
