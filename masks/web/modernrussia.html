  • Type:Изолирующий Противогаз ИП-4М
  • Translation: Insulator Gas Mask IP-4M
  • Aliases: n/a
  • Date: c. mid 1980s-early 1990s
  • Frequency: Rare
  • Country: Russia
  • Use: Military
  • Refrence: RU-050
  • Price: $195
  • Notes: The IP-4M is a closed circuit re-breather that could be used in areas where the oxygen level is to low to sustain life. The device worked by rapidly inflating an air bladder with oxygen generated by a chemical reaction in the РП-4 (RP-4) regenerating canister. The bladder was housed inside the masks carrier below the regenerating canister and carried here while in use and not in use. The face piece used with the IP-4M unit is identical to the one used with the IP-6, but was manufactured with black rubber instead of gray.

The IP-6 can be found on the surplus market in complete un-issued condition with all of the proper accessories and manual.
